The Latte Segment

Thank you for being curious enough to check this out! Yes, I threw an ad in your Easter egg. For a book about talking animals.

The Latte Segment is my first published novel. I wanted to capture the strange and at times contradictory mood of growing up with a growing city. The result has a bit of fantasy to it (as much fantasy as talking animals can provide) and, I hope, an interesting character as well.

If you've been a fan of Bojack Horseman, Zootopia, or Vice's Habits comic series, I hope you give my book a try. And if you do, I hope you enjoy it! I'm open to any sorts of comments or questions you have — whether they're about my book or my slightly strange advertising campaign. (But hey, you did get candy out of it!) Feel free to email them to me. I'd appreciate it!

— Zoe Landon
