Contact

The best way to contact me is actually via Twitter. I tend to check it frequently, and the character limit keeps people from rambling. It also means I can answer quesitons publicly, which is sometimes desirable (especially on technical subjects, or really anything not super-personal).

If you do wish to email me for any reason, feel free. Just note that I don't tend to check it as often, and nobody likes a super-long email.

Other social mediums might work, but are unlikely. Those definitely require putting some care into the message.

Don't bother calling me, if you happen to find my number. I never pick up.
